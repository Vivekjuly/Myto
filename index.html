The cobas® c 311 is a mid-size, fully automated discrete clinical chemistry analyser — part of Roche's cobas 4000 platform family. It consolidates routine and special chemistry workloads into a single robust system, handling serum, plasma, urine, CSF, whole blood (HbA1c), and hemolysate with ISE capability for electrolytes.
Up to 300 photometric tests/hour and 450 ISE tests/hour — ideal for busy mid-volume lab workflows
45 onboard test positions with 42 cooled reagent cassettes — broad panel coverage without manual reagent changes
Contact-free ultrasonic mixing and 3-index serum integrity checks (lipemia, hemolysis, icterus) for every sample
Automated sample rerun, dilution, and additional sample loading during continuous operation — no interruptions
Barcoded cobas® reagents with auto-detection of lot numbers, calibration data, and expiry — zero manual entry errors
